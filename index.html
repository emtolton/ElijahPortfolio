About Me

Early Education

I took part in a rigorous course load, including Advanced Placement (AP) and Concurrent Enrollment (CE) classes, which allowed me to earn my Associate's degree before graduating from high school. This strong academic foundation has been key in shaping my approach to challenges and learning.


Current Studies

I've had the opportunity to learn various programming languages and concepts, expanding my technical knowledge and preparing myself for a career in this rapidly evolving field. I'm graduating with a Bacholer's of Computer Science in 2027. I'm excited to continue learning and apply my skills to real-world problems.


A Lifelong Runner

These experiences not only taught me the value of perseverance but also instilled in me a love for competing and constantly improving. Running continues to be a part of my life, helping me stay disciplined and focused in everything I do.
